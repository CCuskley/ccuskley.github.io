Using Look who's talking

Click on a category to start timing. For example, if a meeting kicks off with a man talking, click or tap 'Man'. When the category of the talker switches, click or tap the new category. The overal total talk times in minutes and seconds will show in a table at the top of the screen; click "show data" to see a pie chart and streamgraph of who's talking that will update in real time.

Use the Proportions button to add information about who's in the room (i.e., how many people there are from each group in the meeting or event). Adding propoortions will add a small pie chart showing what the time proportions would be if every person spoke for an equal amount of time.

You can edit the existing categories or add new ones (up to 5). For example, in many contexts the gender of the speaker is interesting, but perhaps you're interested in e.g., how much time a members of marketing vs engineering spend talking in a meeting. Finally, you can use the 'Download' button to save the data when you're done.
