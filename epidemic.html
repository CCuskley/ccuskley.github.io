Containment Game

There are N cells in the population, in a finite space (some of which you can't see). They move about, going about their business, bumping into one another. They can just do this willy nilly or do a few different things:

  • Social distancing: don't come within more than 1 m of any other cell.
  • Self-isolation: If a cell knows its come near another infected cell, it self-isolates.
  • Lock down: All cells self-isolate
  • Testing: testing can either happen for everyone who wants it, or only for those that are symptomatic.
  • Incubation period: The duration of time a cell is infected but asymptomatic
  • Hospitalisation rate: the percentage of infected cells that require hospitalisation
  • Fatality rate: the percentage of infected cells that die
